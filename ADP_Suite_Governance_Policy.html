What is the ADP Suite?

The ADP Suite: Employee Profile Dashboard is a comprehensive data integration system that combines workforce data from ADP Workforce Now with operational and academic performance metrics. It creates a complete profile of onsite staff and provides automated rehire recommendations based on objective, measurable benchmarks.

üéØ Purpose & Goals

Mission: To create a fair, transparent, and data-driven approach to evaluating employee performance and making informed rehiring decisions that serve the best interests of both tutors and scholars.

Primary Objectives

1. Eliminate Subjective Bias in Rehiring Decisions

By establishing clear, measurable benchmarks across four key areas (academic performance, attendance, tutor-scholar rapport, and program concerns), the ADP Suite ensures that rehiring decisions are based on objective data rather than personal opinions or incomplete information.

  • Quantifiable Metrics: All decisions are grounded in numerical thresholds and documented evidence
  • Consistent Standards: The same criteria apply to all employees in equivalent roles
  • Transparent Process: Employees understand what is expected and how they are evaluated
  • Defensible Outcomes: Every decision can be traced back to specific performance data

2. Provide Comprehensive Employee Performance Profiles

The dashboard creates a holistic view of each employee by integrating multiple data sources into a single, accessible profile that tracks performance across time and locations.

Profile Components Include:

  • Demographic and employment information from ADP
  • Historical work locations and role assignments
  • Program concern documentation with timestamps
  • Scholar survey feedback on learning and rapport
  • Academic placement progress data
  • Attendance rates and consistency metrics
  • Rehire eligibility status with supporting context

3. Support Informed Leadership Decision-Making

Program leaders and HR teams gain access to actionable insights that support strategic staffing decisions, performance interventions, and resource allocation.

4. Maintain Historical Accuracy and Compliance

The system preserves a complete, auditable record of employee performance and organizational decision-making that supports compliance, research, and continuous improvement.

Data Retention: The dashboard distinguishes between "Active" employees and those "Archived in ADP," preserving historical data from the 2022-2023 cycle forward.

Data Privacy & Confidentiality

Protected Information:

  • Employee personal data protected by FERPA and employment law
  • Performance records are confidential employment records
  • Scholar data has identifying information removed
  • Medical/accommodation information maintained separately by HR
Violation Consequences: Unauthorized access, sharing, or misuse of ADP Suite data may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
